 Conbook Submissions

️Are you a talented artist or someone who loves creating innovative works? Do you want to showcase your creativity at this year's FurGiV event?

Join Lam in submitting your art for a chance to appear in the official conbook of FurGiV 2026 - Pawful of Chalk!
How to Participate

Submit your artwork, story, poem, or fursuit photos, along with your contact information, name and social media through the form below or via our email: event@furgiv.org 

Submission period: From March 19th to August 16th 

Requirements

Lam welcomes all forms of artworks, stories, poems, and fursuit images, as long as they are related to the theme of the event: Pawful of Chalk

Lam will not accept artworks or images containing inappropriate elements or sensitive topics related to religion, politics, etc…

Remember, you can only submit your own original artworks or photos!
