Who we are

FurGIV (Furry Gathering in Vietnam) is a Vietnamese furry event where furries across the country can meet, connect, and welcome international friends to our home. It is primarily aimed at members of the furry community, people with an interest in animals with human characteristics, but everyone is welcome to attend.

Our mission is to create a safe and inclusive environment where furries can express themselves freely while learning about Vietnamese culture. We strive to celebrate and incorporate Vietnam’s rich traditions into the vibrant, modern culture of the furry fandom.

Debuting in 2024, FurGIV is an event based in a historically rich nation often known as the Land of the Blue Dragon. Its goal is to build a recreational and communal space where Vietnamese and international furries can bond over shared interests and beyond. At FurGIV, we believe that an open mind is key to fostering a joyful, energetic, and positive environment for all.

Our Mascot

Lam the Dragon

Once upon a time, a faespawn birthed from a most unusual union slept soundly beneath a mountain for thousands of years.

Within his slumber, he dreamt of festivities to which he was host to hundreds of fantastical creatures.

Now awakened, the dragon seeks to realize that dream for the merry of many.

To that end, he would begin the gathering here, in Hanoi, where he had dozed for so long.

Lam is energetic, cheerful, confident, sometimes mischievous but most importantly a generous host and a helpful friend.

No matter the type of media he is in, he will always bring with him playfulness, helpfulness with just enough mischiefs to be fun but serious when needed to be.
