What is the nature of this event?

We consider ourselves as a furry gathering with the goal of creating a fun space for the community

Who is this event for?

FurGiV is a Vietnamese furry event for local furries to meet one another and to welcome international friends to our home country

Can we sell food at the event?    

Food will not be sold at the event area, but the event location has a food and beverage area that contains items sold by different restaurants and cafes. The location does not allow bringing outside food or drinks in, and please don't carry them near the fursuiters to avoid accidents!

Will there be charity or fundraising?

Due to the legalities of hosting such activities, there will not be any fundraisers or charitable activities for this year. We are actively working on a solution for this.

Will there be any DJ parties at the event?

There will be no DJ parties in FurGiV's event timeline.

Will there be any benefits for Southerners going to the North?

There will be no additional benefits for Southerners traveling to the event.

Are there any guides for the event and area nearby?

Guides for the event along with suggestions for the area surrounding it will be posted on our Facebook, you can keep yourself updated by following our page.
