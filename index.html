Ton, ton, ton!

Principal Lam and the FurGIV homeroom teachers would like to announce to fellow pupils about this year’s official theme!

Red phoenix flowers drift in the wind, school is in session again! To invoke a shared nostalgia we all have for the years spent on our school benches, “Pawful Of Chalk” has been chosen. With this theme, we hope to create a space for everyone to relive and revisit their student years, albeit with less uniform and more fur!

This time, Lam is here to see you make friends with your classmates, spend time with them during short recesses, and attentively listen to your teachers. May we all make new and joyous memories of our time shared under the FurGIV school’s roof this upcoming September.

Guest of Honor

Chucky

Class, please meet the fantastical, magical freelance artist… Chucky!

Chucky here is a freelance artist who specializes in comics and webtoons. Principal Lam even heard that their specialty is all sorts of medieval fantasy topics, ranging from mundane slice-of-life to grand adventures. In fact, they are hoping that they can create an original series about humans, furries, and fantastical creatures living together in the future.

Needless to say, Principal Lam and the FurGIV School Board are very much looking forward to what they will bring to the class of FurGIV 2026!

Location

FurGIV 2026 - Pawful of Chalk will be taking place on the 19th of September, at Grand Vista Hotel Giang Vo!

 It’s been a long time building up to this point and we could not have made it without all of your support. So mark down your calendars and get ready to take a trip down memory lane back to those classroom memories.
