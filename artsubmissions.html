Art Submissions

Hey you!

Are you a talented artist or someone who loves creating innovative works? Do you want to showcase your creativity at this year's FurGiV event?

Join Lam in submitting your art for a chance to appear in the official conbook of FurGiV 2025 - Paws, Claws, and Sidewalk Stalls!

How to participate?

 How to Participate

- Submit your artwork or fursuit photos, along with your contact information, name and social media via our email: event@furgiv.org 

- Submission period: From May 15th to August 21st 

Requirements

- Lam welcomes all forms of artworks and fursuit images, as long as they are related to the theme of the event: Paws, Claws, and Sidewalk Stalls

- Lam will not accept artworks or images containing inappropriate elements or sensitive topics related to religion, politics, etc…

- Remember, you can only submit your own original artworks or photos!
