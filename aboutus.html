FurGIV

Furry Gathering In Vietnam

FurGIV (Furry Gathering In Vietnam) is a Vietnamese furry event for all furries in the country to meet one another, and to welcome international friends to our home country.

Our mission is to create a safe space and environment for all furries to be able to express themselves while learning about the Vietnamese culture. 

Once upon a time, a faespawn birthed from a most unusual union slept soundly beneath a mountain for thousands of years.
Within his slumber, he dreamt of festivities to which he was host to hundreds of fantastical creatures.
Now awakened, the dragon seeks to realize that dream for the merry of many.
To that end, he would begin the gathering here, in Hanoi, where he had dozed for so long.
